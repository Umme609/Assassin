CHAPTER-2

THE SAVIOUR

It was early morning when Ali woke up. He had a great headache, was on a bed. Free to move in a room. He got up and tried to take a glance of where he is. When he came out the sun’s bright shine tortured his eyes so much that he fell down to ground & started shrieking loudly.

Ali - HELP! SOMEBODY HELP!

A man 6 feet tall came to him running & moved him to the bed again. Ali had a glance with his face. He had blue eyeballs, a mustache, & white in skin colour. His hands were tough as of how a soldier’s should be. His face shape, colour, his dressing made Ali think of where he could be? Then Ali understood that he was a Nemamarian. {A group of hunters who roam covered in long hoods with special knowledge of weapons. Popularly known as Assasins.} He gave Ali a drink. He took a cloth dipped it in the mixture of turmeric powder & hot water, tied it to Ali’s eyes. He then tied the prince’s hands & legs, then said...

Altair - You will feel better. But, till then stay calm okay…

He took a piece of hot coal, made another drink & filtered it into the prince’s mouth. It was very hot which made him unconscious.

Scene - Altair woke him up calling him as Messiah. Ali woke up.

Ali - Who are you & where am I? Why did you bring me here…

Altair - STOP! STOP!!! You will get all your answers when you will be able to walk & see. Till then, you have to stay here & take rest. Okayyy

Lizuki - Hey Altair, Got a Go. You are asked to come to the palace of Eden.

Ali - Oh, so you are Altair.

Lizuki - And I am Lizuki your highness! So, can we go now?

Altair - Your highness?!!

Lizuki - Didn’t you get the information? Well, he is the prince of the kingdom named Basedilm & his father is betrayed to blind. He was ruling his father’s kingdom when YOU! Altair the great! Abducted him for no reason.

Altair - So, they are calling me to know why I kidnapped him.

Lizuki - ...Think so.

Altair - Okay then let’s go!!

Ali - You can’t leave me here. I am also coming with you & stop calling me your highness. My name is Ali. Just Ali.

Altair - look Ali, you need rest… (Interrupted)

Ali - I think I am fine. So, I will join you.

Lizuki - I think we should take him with us.

Ali - Right! I am feeling really fine now.

Altair – But… (Interrupted)

Ali - Altair! Untie me right now. It’s an order.

Altair - Ahh-haa! You aren’t bad in ordering. Haan!

Scene - Altair unties Ali to get him to the palace of Eden. But, suddenly Ali attacks on Altair with a small dagger in his hands. Altair defenses the attack & hits Ali on his head to make him unconscious.

Altair - How was the headshot boy! (Chuckles’)

Lizuki - Ohhh Boy!! Not again.

Altair - He isn’t that tough as I expected how our savior could be.

Lizuki - But is sharp.

Altair - Sharp! He wasn’t even able to make me a small harm.

Lizuki - Really?! (Holding Altair’s hand)

Lizuki - Look you have gotten a cut & you are bleeding!

Altair - Ohhh. How didn’t I feel that?

Lizuki - Now, let’s go!

Altair - yeahhh… (Raising Ali)

Scene - Altair & Lizuki raising Ali to take him to the palace of Eden.

Altair - He’s sooo heavy. (Groans)

Lizuki - Yeahh!

(Drops him with a sigh)

Lizuki - Let me get some help.

Altair - Ok, but don’t make me wait?

Lizuki - Okayyy… (Runs out for help)

(Lizuki looks for someone to get help & calls his friend, Harvey for help)

Lizuki - heyyy… Harv, help me to get the Messiah at the palace of Eden.

Harv - Oh, chief, I am really sorry; I am busy at another task.

Lizuki - It’s an order from Altair himself. Join us right now or you will be badly punished.

Harv - Argh! What a misreble day!

Harv - coming chief!!
